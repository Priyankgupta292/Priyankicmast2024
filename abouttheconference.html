About the Conference

The Shri Ram Murti Smarak College of Engineering and Technology, Bareilly (U.P.) India, will host an International Conference on Mathematics and its Applications in Science and Technology (ICMAST 2024) from August 30 to 31, 2024. In collaboration with the Central University of Punjab, Bathinda, and Pondicherry University, Puducherry, this conference aims to bring together researchers from academia, industries, technology, and R&D organizations worldwide, focusing on mathematics and its applications across various disciplines.The conference will encompass a wide array of topics, including but not limited to differential equations, mathematical modeling, wavelet analysis, control theory, graph theory, fuzzy set theory, simulations, dynamical systems, numerical analysis, solid and fluid mechanics, and computational engineering. Its primary goal is to facilitate discussions among mathematicians and experts from diverse fields, fostering the exchange of ideas and exploring recent advancements in computational and applied mathematics.The conference puts special importance to involve PhD students, post-doctoral fellows, and young researchers from across the globe, emphasizing inclusivity and the nurturing of emerging talent. Furthermore, the conference will feature esteemed keynote speakers specializing in the theme areas, ensuring an enriching and enlightening experience for all attendees.

About SRMS COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING & TECHNOLOGY, BAREILLY, INDIA

Shri Ram Murti Smarak College of Engineering and Technology is a pioneer institute established in 1996 by Shri Dev Murti to advocate the inspiring principles of his father Late Shri Ram Murti Ji- A man with a vision and a man with a mission. The vision of SRMSCET is – “The education of people for professional careers, carried out in a professional way “. The college envisions to inculcate principle oriented technical education among students to build a promising future and to give better human beings to the world. College has an independent residential campus spread over 35 acres of land with all amenities. The college has demonstrated perennial evidence of merit and quality by achieving a respectable position among all parallel private and government colleges of Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University, Lucknow (UP). It is developing by leaps and bounds in terms of infra-structural facilities and human capital not only to fulfill the requirements of the current technological status but also to set itself as teaching and research center of eminence in future.


About Bareilly

Bareilly is situated at 250 km equidistant East from Delhi and West from Lucknow on NH-24. Among the places of interest in the vicinity are Dudhwa National Park, Nainital, Ranikhet, Jim Corbett Park, Akalkhnath Temple and 150 years old Bareilly College, IVRI, CARI, MJPRU are the important institutions located in Bareilly. Bareilly is also famous for its Zari work, Cane Furniture and kohl.


About Department of Mathematics and Statistics, Central University of Punjab, Bathinda, INDIA

The Department of Mathematics and Statistics was established in the year 2015. The department strives to be recognized for excellence among academic institutions in India and abroad. It offers M. Sc. and Ph. D. programs in the area of Mathematics and Statistics. Department focuses on providing a comprehensive curriculum at postgraduate and research level. It provides relevant research and career opportunities in India and abroad. In addition to the faculty’s strong research interests, there is an equally strong desire to provide instruction of the highest quality. Mathematics plays a critical role in our efforts to understand the nature of the physical universe and in the continuing development of our technological society. There is also a long tradition that recognizes the value of mathematics for its aesthetic appeal to the human spirit. Many students of the liberal arts decide to study mathematics for one or both of these reasons. Students also study mathematics in order to develop critical reasoning skills that can significantly contribute to many personal goals. Within the last few years the frenetic pace of research and development in computers and high technology has led to strong new imperatives for more mathematical expertise, and the need to nurture a new generation of mathematically competent men and women. The department offers a six months Certificate Course in Vedic Mathematics with intake of 50 students.


About Department of Mathematics, Pondicherry University, Pondicherry, India

The Department of Mathematics was established in the year 1986 with a primary objective of nurturing students with a strong inclination towards advanced Mathematics, emphasizing both research and teaching. Our department offers a comprehensive range of programs including M.Sc. in Mathematics, Ph.D. programs in Mathematics, and a distinctive 5-year integrated M.Sc. program in Mathematics. We are equipped with state-of-the-art computing facilities and internet resources, ensuring an enriched academic environment for our students. Over the years, we've received valuable support for infrastructure development from the Department of Science and Technology (DST) under the FIST program during 2009-14, as well as the University Grants Commission (UGC) through the Special Assistance Programme (SAP) at both the DRS-I level (2009-14) and the DRS-II level (2015-20). The National Board for Higher Mathematics (NBHM) also extends its support for the acquisition of Mathematics-related library resources.



Our distinguished faculty members are actively engaged in both national and international research projects, contributing significantly to the field of Mathematics. In terms of research focus, our department excels in various key areas, including Algebra, Graph Theory, Hydrodynamic Stability, Number Theory, Differential Equations, Numerical Analysis, Topological Dynamics, Wavelet Theory and Applications, Fractional Calculus, Integral Transforms, Complex Analysis, and Special Functions. Our commitment to excellence in both education and research makes the Department of Mathematics a hub for cutting-edge mathematical exploration and academic growth.
