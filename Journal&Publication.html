Publication Opportunities

High-quality full-length papers submitted and selected for presentation at the “International Conference on Mathematics and its Applications in Science and Technology (ICMAST-2024)” will undergo a double-blind review process by a minimum of two potential reviewers. The level of plagiarism in all submitted papers should not exceed 20%. Authors are required to adhere to the guidelines specified by the respective journal, and the final decision rests with the Editor-in-Chief (EIC). Papers accepted by the Guest Editorial Team will be recommended for publication in one of the relevant journals listed below, which include Web of Science, Scopus, or other peer-reviewed journals. Authors must also cover Article Processing Charges (only if applicable/requiredby the respective journals), in addition to the registration fee.

  • Communications in Combinatorics and Optimization (Indexed: ESCI & Scopus)
  • Proceedings of the Jangjeon Mathematical Society (Indexed: Scopus)
  • SRMS Journal of Mathematical Sciences (Peer Reviewed)
